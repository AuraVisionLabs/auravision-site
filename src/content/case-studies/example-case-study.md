## The Challenge

The client's manufacturing line relied on manual visual inspection, creating bottlenecks and inconsistent quality control across three production facilities.

## Our Approach

We designed and deployed a custom computer vision pipeline using edge-deployed models, enabling real-time defect detection without cloud latency.

## The Results

- **80%** reduction in manual QA time
- **99.2%** defect detection accuracy
- **3×** throughput increase on the production line
